Starting Out:

  • Find out who out of your class is going to be a leader (there needs to be exactly 6 people)
  • WARNING: Some people may already be leaders, check the Class Information tab to find out!
  • After leaders are chosen, people who aren't leaders must flock to a leader (try to distribute the non-leaders as evenly as possible)
  • Now, in order of thos on the list of leaders, pick a country! Be warned though, countries have different strengths and weaknesses
  • After every leader has chosen their civilization, have them email me: their civilization, a new civilization name, a summary of what their civilization is about
    email me at daniel.krawciw@s.stemk12.org
  • Every civilization now recieves 5 gold and what their civization would earn in one turn
    You can find how much one would earn in one turn by going to the Calculator tab, and adding your civilization's bonus (if it has one)

Special Buildings:


  • Unlike normal buildings, there are tiers to special buildings
  • Also, when you start building a special building, you can not switch to a different type if special building
  • Once you have completed the tier ladder for a specific special building though, you can move on to a different one.
  • Also, with special buildings, you can only pick one building upgrade for each tier of building
